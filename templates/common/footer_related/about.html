About Me

Hi, and welcome to my blog.

I’m a Bulgarian mom living in Vienna with my husband and toddler. After more than a decade abroad, Vienna has truly become home — a place where I balance family life, work, and the simple things that make everyday living enjoyable.

Here, I share glimpses of my life as a mom and expat — from cooking homemade meals and keeping a cozy home, to exploring Vienna’s parks, events, and little corners with my son. I also love sharing small pieces of my personal style, the occasional outfit of the day, and how I make time for myself — skincare, a quiet moment, or a walk through the city.

This blog is a mix of stories, routines, and reflections — nothing overcomplicated, just life as it happens. I hope you find something here that feels familiar, inspiring, or simply pleasant to read.
